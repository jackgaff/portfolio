About Me

Hi, I'm Jack, a computer engineering student at the University of Michigan. I'm passionate about delving into the intricacies of low-level coding and computer architecture. Exploring the foundational aspects of computing has always intrigued me.

My academic journey in computer engineering has been an immersive experience, where I've delved into subjects such as digital logic, microprocessors, and system design. I find joy in deciphering the complexities that govern the core functioning of computers and systems.

Besides web development, I have many other hobbies and interests that keep me busy and happy. I love reading books, and watching all types of sports. I also enjoy playing golf, watching movies, listening to music, and traveling. Some of my favorite things are football, Star Wars, The Masters, 60s music, and the Fast and Furious franchise.

My goal is to become a full-stack web developer and work on projects that can make a positive impact on the world. I am always looking for new opportunities and challenges that can help me grow as a web developer and as a person.
